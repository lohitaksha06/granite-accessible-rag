Grocery checkout guidance (self-service or cashier)

- Choose a checkout: cashier lane or self-checkout.
- If self-checkout is available, scan each item barcode one at a time.
- Place scanned items in the bagging area if required.
- If an item does not scan, use the “Help” option or call an attendant.
- For produce, use the lookup menu to select the item type.
- When finished, confirm you have scanned all items.
- Select a payment method: card, cash (if supported), or mobile payment.
- Follow on-screen prompts to complete payment.
- Collect your receipt if you need it.
- Take your items and move to the packing area.
