Bank account help (forms and guidance)

- Decide what you need: open an account, update details, or get statements.
- Prepare required documents (ID and proof of address if requested).
- If you are using a kiosk, select the exact service from the menu.
- Read each form field slowly and complete it one step at a time.
- If you do not understand a term, ask for a plain-language explanation.
- Double-check spelling of your name, phone number, and address.
- Before submitting, review the summary screen if available.
- If you feel unsure, request staff review before final submission.
- Keep your receipt or reference number for follow-up.
- For accessibility needs, request an accessible format or assistance option.
